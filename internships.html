NIST's Summer High School Intern Program (SHIP) offers an eight-week, unpaid internship for U.S. high school juniors and seniors interested in science. Participants work on individual research projects alongside NIST scientists. The application period for SHIP 2024 opens on December 1, 2023, and closes in mid-February. Eligible students with a minimum GPA of 3.0 must commit to the full program. While unpaid, the program provides valuable research experience. Skills in Python, physics, electronics, LabView, AutoDesk Inventor, Java, AI, SQL, Visual Studio, web development, and more are beneficial. Students are responsible for their housing and transportation.
